Manifest

TechParty is an event that longs to promote technology in an informal and collaborative manner.

Since the first edition, the event remains with no cost for those who attend it. In the begining, the event used to happen during the weekend once in each semester of the year, in this weekend, courses about computer science were ministered to all kinds of users. Due to some unexpected facts, the event did not happen in 2013.

In 2014, students from Faccat's campus, focusing in offering a better knowledge to a bigger amount of people, decided to realize the event again, but now, in a different mode.

TechParty became an Yearly event, during a full week and hosting two talks per night. A very attractive coffee break was offered in order to promote networking between the participants. The event kept encouraging students from the Campus to talk about trending technologies and methodologies. The event had more than 700 participants during the 5 days it happened.

Rango Break, the beggining. In 2015, TechParty had the same vibe, but with a bigger audience and excitement among the participants during the TechParty week. Any news? Yes! The coffee break goes out and a brand new event(an event hosting an event) comes to life: Rango Break.

Now, the idea is to turn the snack time much more interesting. Rango Break is a moment where, daily, after the end of both talks, snacks(Rango is a Brazilian slang standind for simple kinds of food) are offered to participants. Diffent kinds of snack were offered in each day of TechParty. Hot dogs, cupcakes, sandwiches and much more. All this snacks are provided with no cost to participants due to the companies that sponsor the event.

What about 2016? We hope, once again, to realize a big event and improve what is good! As soon as the 2015 edition finished, we started planning the event for 2016. We are always listening our public to understand what they want to know/learn about, and as soon as we discover the needs of the public, we search for speakers who can talk about the required subjects. We need your participation to make the TechParty 2016 the most incredible so far!
